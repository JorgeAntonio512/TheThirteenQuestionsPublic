## Privacy Policy

**The Thirteen Questions**

**Effective date:** January 2026

The Thirteen Questions is designed to respect user privacy through intentional restraint.

### Information we collect

We do **not** collect, store, or transmit personal data.

Specifically:

* No accounts are created
* No personal identifiers are requested
* No answers are stored permanently
* No data is transmitted off the device

All responses entered into the app exist only in memory during an active session and are discarded when the session ends or the app is closed.

---

### Analytics and tracking

The app does not use analytics, advertising trackers, or third-party data collection services.

Basic crash reporting may be enabled solely to improve app stability. This information does not include user-entered content.

---

### Data storage

* No persistent storage is used
* No cloud services are used
* No data is backed up or synced
* No data can be reviewed after a session ends

Your answers stay on your device and are not shared.

---

### Third-party services

The app does not integrate with third-party services that collect or process user data.
